Wefilling Privacy Policy (iOS)

Effective Date: November 12, 2025
Platform: iOS App Store Version
App Version: 1.0.0

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: University email for account verification
  • Display Name: Your chosen nickname
  • Profile Photo: Optional profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Apple Sign In or Google Sign In credentials

1.2 Content You Create

  • Posts: Text and images you share
  • Comments: Your responses to posts
  • Messages: Direct messages with other users
  • Meetup Information: Events you create or join

1.3 Usage Information

  • App Interactions: Features you use and how you use them
  • Device Information: iOS version, device model, app version
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Push Tokens: For sending notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functions

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Displaying your content to other users
  • Facilitating meetups and social connections
  • Sending notifications about app activities

2.2 Safety and Security

  • University email verification
  • Preventing spam and abuse
  • Investigating reported content
  • Maintaining community standards

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyzing app performance
  • Fixing bugs and crashes
  • Developing new features
  • Understanding user preferences

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3.1 With Other Users

  • Your posts, comments, and profile information (as per your privacy settings)
  • Your participation in meetups
  • Your friend connections (visible to mutual friends)

3.2 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, database, storage, and notifications
  • Apple: App Store services and Apple Sign In

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

  • Data is stored on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Servers are located in secure data centers
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your profile and posts in the app
  • Edit Information: Update your profile and settings
  • Delete Content: Remove your posts and comments
  • Privacy Settings: Control who can see your content

5.2 Account Deletion

  • You can delete your account at any time in app settings
  • Account deletion removes all your personal data
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics
  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

6. Children's Privacy

Wefilling is intended for users 17 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If we discover that a child under 17 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Third-Party Services

9.1 Apple Sign In

When you use Apple Sign In, Apple's privacy policy also applies. We receive minimal information from Apple, typically just a unique identifier and optionally your email.

9.2 Google Sign In

When you use Google Sign In, Google's privacy policy also applies. We access only basic profile information necessary for account creation.

9.3 Firebase Services

We use Firebase for backend services. Google's privacy policy for Firebase applies to data processing by these services.

11. Legal Basis (for EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your information includes:

  • Contract: To provide the app services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and ensure safety
  • Consent: For optional features like notifications
